Heres a guide on how to use the tool!

Get Started

When you access the tool, first decide which month you have events that you want to plan for. You can click the arrow left to have the descending months and the arrow right for the ascending months. Take reference from the photo.

Select days

Hover your mouse over any of the days that is highlighted darkblue. The number will increase in size indicating that your mouse cursor is on it. As you click on the selected day, there will a shadow box displaying after clicking. Take reference from the photo.

Add your plans

After ensuring there is a shadow box on the selected day, scroll down until u see a button and the word empty (first time using). Click on the button and a box will pop up with 3 main features. The name of the task, the start time and the end time. Fill those 3 inputs and press on the submit button. Take reference from the photo.

Summary

The added task will be shown in the empty box, it will stay there until you delete it. You can delete it by click on anywhere within the highlighted area and there will be a alert asking if you want to delete it. Just press yes and the task will be deleted. Take reference from the photo.

Keep track of days

After adding your tasks, you do not need to worry about forgetting which the day with the tasks as those day will be highlighted with a darkblue outline. This indicates that you have a task on that day. This can used as a reminder for the users. Take reference from the photo.

Now that you are familiarised with how the calendar works. Click the button below to get started!
